What They Do

Vizient is the largest member-owned healthcare services company in the U.S. The company combines group purchasing (GPO), data analytics, advisory consulting, and supply chain management. Vizient serves 69% of acute care hospitals, 97% of academic medical centers, and 35%+ of ambulatory providers across the country.
