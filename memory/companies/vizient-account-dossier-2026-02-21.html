Quick Take

Vizient is the nation's largest provider-driven healthcare performance improvement company, serving 97% of U.S. academic medical centers and managing $156B in annual purchasing volume. Following their 2024 Kaufman Hall acquisition, they're pivoting from pandemic-era stabilization to an aggressive AI-driven digital transformation strategy with a "healthcare reset" agenda for 2026.

What They Do

Vizient is the largest member-owned healthcare services company in the U.S., combining group purchasing (GPO), data analytics, advisory consulting, and supply chain management to help hospitals and health systems reduce costs while improving quality. They serve 69% of acute care hospitals and 35%+ of ambulatory care providers.
