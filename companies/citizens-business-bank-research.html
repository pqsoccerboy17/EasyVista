What They Do

Citizens Business Bank is a California-focused commercial bank serving small to medium-sized businesses. Their core offerings span business banking, commercial real estate, commercial lending, equipment loans, treasury management, specialized industry banking (including dairy, livestock, and agribusiness), home lending, and wealth management through their CitizensTrust division ($15M in FY2025 revenue).

They serve California's key economic corridors — Inland Empire, Central Valley, Orange County, Los Angeles, San Diego, and (post-merger) the Bay Area and Silicon Valley.
