# FlowSync Onboarding Email Sequence

**Product:** FlowSync -- Workflow automation for growing teams
**Sequence type:** Onboarding drip (post-signup)
**Goal:** Drive activation and reduce time-to-value

---

## Email 1: Welcome & Quick Win

- **Subject:** Welcome to FlowSync -- build your first workflow in 3 minutes
- **Send day:** Day 0 (immediately after signup)
- **Key message:** Thank the user for signing up. Reinforce the core value prop -- FlowSync eliminates manual handoffs between tools. Point them to the guided setup wizard, which walks them through connecting their first two apps and creating a simple workflow.
- **CTA:** "Create your first workflow" (links to onboarding wizard)

---

## Email 2: First Value Moment

- **Subject:** Your team just saved 2 hours this week (here's how to save more)
- **Send day:** Day 3
- **Key message:** Reference the workflow they created (or nudge them if they haven't). Show a concrete time-savings metric based on their usage. Introduce the template library with 50+ pre-built workflows for common use cases like lead routing, support ticket escalation, and approval chains.
- **CTA:** "Browse workflow templates" (links to template gallery)

---

## Email 3: Social Proof & Team Expansion

- **Subject:** How Carta's ops team cut handoff time by 60%
- **Send day:** Day 7
- **Key message:** Share a short customer story showing a team similar to theirs that achieved measurable results. Highlight the multiplier effect of adding teammates -- workflows get more powerful when the whole team participates. Include a one-click invite link.
- **CTA:** "Invite your team" (links to team settings with pre-filled invite)

---

## Email 4: Power Feature Introduction

- **Subject:** You're ready for conditional logic -- here's why it matters
- **Send day:** Day 14
- **Key message:** Now that they have basic workflows running, introduce conditional branching and multi-step automation. Use a specific example relevant to their industry or connected tools. Position this as the unlock that turns simple automations into real business logic.
- **CTA:** "Try conditional workflows" (links to tutorial with interactive sandbox)

---

## Email 5: Upgrade / Commitment

- **Subject:** Your trial ends in 3 days -- lock in your workflows
- **Send day:** Day 25 (3 days before trial ends)
- **Key message:** Summarize their usage stats: workflows created, time saved, tasks automated. Frame the paid plan as protecting the value they've already built. Highlight what they'd lose if they downgrade (workflow history, team seats, conditional logic). Offer a limited-time annual discount for immediate conversion.
- **CTA:** "Upgrade to keep your workflows running" (links to billing page with discount applied)

---

## Sequence Notes

- **Suppression rules:** Skip Email 2 if user hasn't completed onboarding by Day 3 -- send a re-engagement nudge instead
- **Personalization:** Dynamic merge fields for user name, company name, connected apps, and usage metrics
- **A/B test priority:** Subject lines on Email 1 and Email 5 (highest-leverage touchpoints)
- **Exit criteria:** User converts to paid at any point -- move to "New Customer" sequence
